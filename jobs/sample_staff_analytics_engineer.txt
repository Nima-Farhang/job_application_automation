About the job

We are currently looking for a Staff Analytics Engineer in New Zealand.

This is a high-impact individual contributor role designed for a seasoned analytics expert who thrives on building scalable, reliable data systems that empower entire organizations. You will play a critical role in establishing a unified data foundation, enabling teams across product, finance, marketing, and revenue to make faster, data-driven decisions with confidence.

Accountabilities
- Design and implement a scalable, reliable analytics architecture that serves as the single source of truth across all business functions
- Own and evolve the data modeling layer
- Lead the definition and standardization of canonical metrics
- Audit and refactor existing data models
- Enable self-service analytics
- Mentor and influence data practices across the team

Requirements
- 7-10+ years of experience in analytics engineering, data engineering, or a related field
- Advanced proficiency in SQL
- Deep experience with data warehousing technologies
- Strong expertise in dbt
- Strong stakeholder management skills
- High level of autonomy, ownership, and systems thinking
